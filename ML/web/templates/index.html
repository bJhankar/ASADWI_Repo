About AquaSense

Our mission is to make water quality assessment accessible and accurate for everyone

The Problem

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet billions of people worldwide still lack this essential resource. Traditional water testing methods are often expensive, time-consuming, and not readily available in many regions.

Our Solution

AquaSense combines machine learning with water science to provide instant predictions about water quality, geological formations, and lithology analysis - helping communities, researchers, and organizations make informed decisions about water sources.

Our Features

Comprehensive water analysis powered by advanced algorithms

Water Quality Prediction

Predict water potability based on various parameters such as pH, hardness, solids, chloramines, and more with high accuracy.

Geological Analysis

Identify geological formations and their potential impact on groundwater quality and availability in different regions.

Lithology Prediction

Analyze soil layers and compositions to determine aquifer characteristics and water-bearing potential of different formations.
